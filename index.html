The Plain Truth: Minnesota's Somali Daycare Heist – Billions Stolen, Borders Mocked, Heritage Erased

By The Watchman

America, wake up and rage! In Minnesota, a massive Somali-led daycare fraud ring has looted taxpayers for billions – ghost centers with no kids, billing millions for phantom meals and care. The Feeding Our Future scam alone stole $250 million in COVID aid, part of a web potentially topping $9-10 billion across welfare, Medicaid, and housing.

Whistleblowers spotted empty shacks claiming 120 kids but showing zero; one "center" raked in $1.9 million from a windowless dump. Defendants – overwhelmingly Somali – faked enrollments, staged fake break-ins to hide tracks, and reopened scams under new names. State auditors flagged this as early as 2014, but Democrat enablers like Governor Walz buried warnings, terrified of "Islamophobia" accusations while courting bloc votes.

Where did the money go? Not to hungry American kids. Fraudsters splurged on Porsche fleets, waterfront mansions, jewelry, island honeymoons – and wired millions overseas. Properties bought in Kenya, Turkey, and Somalia itself.

Conservative probes and federal sources allege some cash funneled to al-Shabaab terror networks taxing Somali regions – remittances swelling while our veterans starve. Treasury's investigating, but mainstream outlets downplay, claiming "no solid link." Baloney! This is organized theft, draining sound money principles to fund foreign chaos.

And the insults pile on: amid this invasion-by-fraud, Minnesota swapped its historic flag for a blue-and-star design mocked nationwide as aping Somalia's – or its regional banners. Critics howl it's a surrender symbol, scrubbing American heritage for globalist virtue-signaling under Walz's watch. Meanwhile, certain radical Somali-born politicians in Congress – representing the epicenter – saw personal wealth rocket to $30 million ranges via obscure disclosures and husband's ventures, all while "defending" the community as fraud exploded. Coincidence? Or enablers profiting from the rot?

This is tyranny: open borders gaming welfare magnets, eroding sovereignty, mocking self-reliance. God condemns unjust scales (Proverbs 11:1); nations ignoring justice court ruin. America First demands: deport exploiters, claw back every dime, prosecute enablers high and low. Seal borders, shrink the beast, restore heritage before radicals turn the heartland into a third-world outpost.

Awaken! Demand raids, freezes, justice. Point to Christ, table-flipper of corruption. The cry thunders: reclaim the republic now!
