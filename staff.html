The Voices of the Cry

These are the spirits that speak through our pages — not men of title, but voices forged in faith, fire, and frontier grit. They write under assumed names so the truth may stand alone, unclouded by fame or faction.

The Watchman

Stands upon the wall when all others slumber — and speaks what others dare not whisper.

The Old Prophet

Reads the handwriting on the wall before the wall is even built.

The Sentinel

Spies the wolf in shepherd's clothing and laughs while the flock still grazes.

The Frontier Builder

Turns lightning into lamps and chaos into workshops — one stubborn invention at a time.

The Working Patriot

Knows the price of liberty is paid in calluses, not speeches.

The Cry is sounded by many hands, yet one purpose.
