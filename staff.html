Our Staff

The voices behind The Watchman's Cry are drawn from various walks of life, united by a common commitment to truth, reason, and the public good. While we maintain anonymity to focus attention on ideas rather than personalities, we present these personas to represent the spirit of our publication.

The Watchman

The Watchman serves as the primary voice of this publication, taking up the mantle of vigilance and truth-telling. Drawing from a background in history, philosophy, and public affairs, The Watchman provides the opening dispatches and oversees the editorial direction of the publication.

The role of the watchman is ancient and sacred—to stand guard, to observe, to warn, and to guide. In this tradition, The Watchman seeks to fulfill the duty of speaking truth to power and providing clarity in times of confusion.

The Chronicler

The Chronicler is responsible for the Prophetic Parallels section, examining historical patterns and drawing lessons from the past. With expertise in historical research and analysis, The Chronicler helps readers understand how the challenges of today relate to those faced by previous generations.

History, as The Chronicler reminds us, does not repeat itself, but it often rhymes. By understanding these patterns, we can better navigate the complexities of our own time.

The Scribe

The Scribe compiles The Plain Truth section, focusing on factual analysis and evidence-based reporting. With a commitment to accuracy and clarity, The Scribe presents information in a straightforward manner, allowing readers to form their own conclusions.

The Scribe's duty is to record events and analyze them with precision, free from the distortions of partisan rhetoric or ideological bias.

The Counselor

The Counselor responds to questions in the Common Man's Counsel section, applying the principles of common sense to the concerns and questions of readers. Drawing from practical wisdom and reasoned analysis, The Counselor seeks to provide guidance that is both thoughtful and accessible.

The Counselor believes that wisdom is not the exclusive domain of the learned or the powerful, but is available to all who seek it with an open mind and a willing heart.

While these personas represent the spirit and approach of our publication, The Watchman's Cry is ultimately a collaborative effort, drawing from the wisdom and insights of our entire readership community.
