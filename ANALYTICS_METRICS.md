# Analytics Metrics to Track

## Core Traffic Metrics

### 1. **Page Views**
- Total page views across all pages
- Page views per page/URL
- Page views over time (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Why**: Understand overall site traffic and which content is most popular

### 2. **Unique Visitors**
- Total unique visitors (based on IP or session)
- New vs returning visitors
- **Why**: Measure actual audience size, not just page loads

### 3. **Sessions**
- Total sessions
- Average session duration
- Pages per session
- **Why**: Understand how users interact with your site

## Page-Level Metrics

### 4. **Top Pages**
- Most viewed pages
- Least viewed pages
- **Why**: Identify popular content and pages that need improvement

### 5. **Entry Pages**
- First page users land on
- **Why**: Understand how users discover your site

### 6. **Exit Pages**
- Last page users view before leaving
- **Why**: Identify where users are dropping off

## Traffic Sources

### 7. **Referrers**
- Where traffic is coming from (direct, search engines, social media, other sites)
- Top referring domains
- **Why**: Understand how users find your site

### 8. **Search Terms** (if available)
- Keywords users searched to find your site
- **Why**: Understand what content users are looking for

## User Behavior

### 9. **Bounce Rate**
- Percentage of single-page sessions
- **Why**: Measure engagement quality

### 10. **Time on Page**
- Average time users spend on each page
- **Why**: Measure content engagement

### 11. **Scroll Depth**
- How far users scroll on pages
- **Why**: Understand if users are reading full content

## Technical Metrics

### 12. **Device Types**
- Desktop, mobile, tablet breakdown
- **Why**: Understand your audience's preferred devices

### 13. **Browsers**
- Browser usage (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
- **Why**: Ensure compatibility with your audience

### 14. **Operating Systems**
- OS breakdown (Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, etc.)
- **Why**: Technical compatibility insights

## Geographic Metrics

### 15. **Countries/Regions**
- Where your visitors are located
- **Why**: Understand your geographic reach

### 16. **Cities** (optional, less privacy-friendly)
- Major cities where visitors are located
- **Why**: Local audience insights

## Time-Based Metrics

### 17. **Peak Hours**
- Times of day with most traffic
- **Why**: Understand when your audience is most active

### 18. **Peak Days**
- Days of week with most traffic
- **Why**: Content scheduling insights

### 19. **Traffic Trends**
- Growth/decline over time
- Seasonal patterns
- **Why**: Track site growth and identify trends

## Engagement Metrics

### 20. **Returning Visitors**
- Percentage of repeat visitors
- **Why**: Measure site loyalty and engagement

### 21. **Click-Through Rate** (for links)
- If tracking outbound links
- **Why**: Measure link engagement

## Privacy Considerations

- **Don't track**: Personal information, IP addresses (store hashed), exact locations
- **Do track**: Aggregated data, anonymized metrics, general patterns
- **Comply with**: GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
- **Best practice**: Use hashed IPs, respect Do Not Track headers, provide opt-out options
