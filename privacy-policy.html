Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

The Watchman's Cry: Common Sense Reborn is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data. We believe in transparency, minimal data collection, and your right to privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our content and better serve our readers.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Page Views: Which pages you visit and when
  • Device Information: Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet), browser type, and operating system
  • Geographic Data: Country-level location (derived from IP address)
  • Time on Page: How long you spend on each page
  • Referrer Information: The website that directed you to our site (if applicable)

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • IP addresses (we hash them immediately)
  • Cookies or tracking cookies
  • Precise location data (only country-level)
  • Any information that could identify you personally

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for website analytics to:

  • Understand which content is most popular
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify technical issues or broken links
  • Understand our audience demographics at an aggregate level

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Privacy Protections

We implement several measures to protect your privacy:

IP Address Hashing

Your IP address is immediately hashed using SHA-256 encryption before storage. This means we cannot identify you or your specific location from the stored data. The hashed value is used only to distinguish unique visitors.

No Cookies

We do not use cookies. Session information is stored only in your browser's sessionStorage, which is automatically cleared when you close your browser.

Do Not Track

We respect your browser's "Do Not Track" setting. If you have Do Not Track enabled, we will not collect any analytics data from your visit.

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only what is necessary for basic website analytics. We do not collect personal information, browsing history from other sites, or any data that could be used to identify you.

Data Storage and Retention

Analytics data is stored securely in our database. We retain this data for up to 24 months to allow for year-over-year comparisons and trend analysis. After this period, data is automatically deleted.

All data is stored on secure servers with appropriate access controls. Only authorized administrators can access analytics data, and only for the purposes described in this policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

Right to Access

You have the right to know what data we have collected about you. However, since we hash IP addresses and do not collect personal information, we cannot identify specific data tied to you as an individual.

Right to Deletion

You may request deletion of your data. However, because we hash IP addresses and do not store personal identifiers, we cannot identify specific records to delete. If you have concerns, please contact us using the information below.

Right to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of analytics tracking by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings. We respect this setting and will not collect any data when it is enabled.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Our data collection practices are designed to minimize privacy impact while still providing useful website analytics.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage. Supabase complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations.
  • Vercel: For website hosting. Vercel processes requests but does not have access to our analytics data.
  • IP Geolocation API: We may use a free IP geolocation service to determine country-level location. This service does not store your IP address.

These services are used only for the technical operation of our website and analytics system. They do not have permission to use your data for their own purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Submit page on our website. We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

For privacy-related requests, please include "Privacy Policy" in your message so we can address your concern appropriately.
